So, Most of the inhabitants of Tura loves playing sports, But not as much as they like Music. The Garos (Inhabitants of Tura) loves music just like the other people do, they often have music festival where everyone can participate and enjoy the moment. They have competitions every year which is held in some spacious place, the common known competiton is called "The Battle Rock" where the competition is among the different rock bands. They usually have Festivals in winter; also called Winter Festivals, this festival is held in all the places of Meghalaya every year.

Now, coming back to sports, the Garos plays all types of sports, but most of the Garos like playing cricket or football besides some Garos plays basketball, volleyball or other types of indoor sports like Badminton or Table-tennis. There are five known stadiums in Tura which are Dikki Bandi Stadium also known as P.A. Sangma stadium, Chandmary mini-stadium, Don-Bosco mini-stadium, District indoor sports Hall and Cricket Stadium. Also, in P.A. Sangma Stadium the gym is included inside the stadium where there are enough equipments. In these stadiums anyone can participate by paying the fees to the stadium incharge anytime.
